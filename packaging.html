Go Greener, Go Clearn With NanoQuinn ™ Packaging!

NanoQuinn™ offers sustainable and environmentally friendly alternatives to conventional plastic packaging. With the increasing concerns about plastic waste and its impact on the environment, it has become crucial to rethink the way we manufacture, use, and manage plastics.

At NanoQuinn, we are committed to addressing these challenges and providing innovative packaging solutions. Let's explore our offerings in detail:

Starch and PBAT Blend Bioplastic Granules

Conventional plastic materials derived from petrochemicals pose a significant threat to the environment. Our Starch and PBAT Blend Bioplastic Granules offer a sustainable alternative. Starch, a renewable resource, forms the basis of our bioplastic, providing biodegradability, film-forming properties, and low cost. By blending starch with Polybutylene adipate terephthalate (PBAT), we enhance the mechanical and barrier properties of the material, particularly its water barrier properties. This blend combines the flexibility and thermal stability of PBAT with the biodegradability of aliphatic polyesters, making it an ideal choice for packaging applications.

Cost-effective and New Age Formulation Technologies

Our Rajkot manufacturing plant is equipped with advanced extrusion compounding technology, enabling the production of Starch and PBAT-based bioplastic granules at a large scale. By utilizing specific polymer additives and compatibilizers, we have not only reduced the cost of the final products but also improved their desired properties. The manufacturing process aligns with plastic waste management rules, ensuring the recyclability, biodegradability, and compostability of the packaging products. NanoQuinn's formulation technologies bring a game-changing scenario to the biopolymers domain.

Water-Based Emulsion Coated Packaging Solutions

In our Ahmedabad Project, we specialize in water-based emulsion coated packaging solutions, specifically tailored to meet our clients' needs. We recognize the importance of repulpability, recyclability, biodegradability, and compostability in paper and paperboard packaging. Our coatings, applied to fiber-based substrates, improve functional properties such as reduced water absorbance, enhanced surface finish, gloss, printability, and dimensional stability. They also provide desirable barrier properties for food packaging applications, including oxygen, aroma, and oil resistance. By using our coated fiber-based substrates.

NanoQuinn's Water-Based Emulsion Coating Formulations

Our Water-Based Emulsion Coating Formulations are designed to address the challenges of waste management and provide sustainable packaging options. These formulations have been developed to outperform existing coating chemicals in terms of technical properties, cost-effectiveness, and performance. Our team of experienced professionals and scientists, with extensive knowledge in the paper and paperboard packaging sector, is dedicated to delivering ultimate client satisfaction. We combine technology, process, and application expertise to provide you with exceptional packaging solutions.
